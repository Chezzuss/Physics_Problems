# Task 08 – Lorentz Force

## Problem Statement

Find Lorentz force.

## Theory

$$
F = qvB
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
F = 2 \times 10^{-19} \cdot 10^6 \cdot 0.5
$$

$$
F = 1 \times 10^{-13} \text{ N}
$$

## Final Result

$$
F = 1 \times 10^{-13} \text{ N}
$$

## Interpretation

Force is perpendicular to velocity.
