# Task 04 – Force Comparison

## Problem Statement

Compare electric and gravitational forces between an electron and a proton separated by $r = 5.3 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m}$.

## Theory

Electric force:

$$
F_e = k \frac{e^2}{r^2}
$$

Gravitational force:

$$
F_g = G \frac{m_e m_p}{r^2}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Ratio:

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{k e^2}{G m_e m_p}
$$

Substitute values:

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} \approx 2.3 \times 10^{39}
$$

## Final Result

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} \approx 10^{39}
$$

## Interpretation

Electric force dominates gravity at atomic scale.
