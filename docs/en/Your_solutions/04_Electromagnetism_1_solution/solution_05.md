# Task 05 – Field Levitation

## Problem Statement

Find electric field required to levitate a proton.

## Theory

Equilibrium condition:

$$
qE = mg
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
E = \frac{mg}{q}
$$

Substitute:

$$
E = \frac{1.67 \times 10^{-27} \cdot 9.8}{1.6 \times 10^{-19}}
$$

$$
E \approx 1.02 \times 10^{-7} \text{ N/C}
$$

## Final Result

$$
E \approx 1.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ N/C}
$$

## Interpretation

A very small electric field is enough to counter gravity at microscopic scale.
