# Task 09 – Vector Lorentz Force

## Problem Statement

Find magnetic force magnitude.

## Theory

$$
\vec F = q (\vec v \times \vec B)
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Compute cross product:

$$
\vec v \times \vec B =
\begin{pmatrix}
\hat i & \hat j & \hat k \\
2 & -4 & 1 \\
1 & 2 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
= (4 - 2)\hat i - ( -2 - 1)\hat j + (4 + 4)\hat k
$$

$$
= 2\hat i + 3\hat j + 8\hat k
$$

Magnitude:

$$
|\vec F| = q \sqrt{2^2 + 3^2 + 8^2}
$$

$$
= 2 \times 10^{-19} \cdot \sqrt{77}
$$

## Final Result

$$
|\vec F| \approx 1.76 \times 10^{-18} \text{ N}
$$

## Interpretation

Magnetic force depends on vector orientation.
