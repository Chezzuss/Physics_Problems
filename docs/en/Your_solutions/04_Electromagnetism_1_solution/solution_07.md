# Task 07 – Cyclotron Motion

## Problem Statement

An electron accelerated by $5000 \text{ V}$ enters a magnetic field $B = 0.1 \text{ T}$. Find radius.

## Theory

Energy:

$$
qV = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
$$

Magnetic force:

$$
qvB = \frac{mv^2}{r}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Solve for velocity:

$$
v = \sqrt{\frac{2qV}{m}}
$$

Radius:

$$
r = \frac{mv}{qB}
$$

Combine:

$$
r = \frac{\sqrt{2mV/q}}{B}
$$

## Final Result

$$
r \approx 0.075 \text{ m}
$$

## Interpretation

Stronger magnetic field reduces radius.
